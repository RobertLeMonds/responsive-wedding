The Bride and Groom

NICHOLE

She is a Texan with pretty teeth. She's not the "Glad it's Friday" but the "Glad it's Today/Love your life 24/7' kind of gal. Her hobbies are ice cream, falling off bikes and organizing random people's things. She's usually found with her four-pawed sidekick who has been known to 'disappear' on occasion. Jesus know this little sheep by name and she's proud of it!

ROBERT

Using only a hoe and a large jar of olive oil, I once single-handedly defended a small village in the African Tundra from a horde of ferocious army hyenas. I play bluegrass violin, I was scouted by every Cleveland sports franchise but was kicked out due my lack of athletic ability, I am the subject of awkward situations. When I'm bored, I read nerdy schoolgirl anime. I enjoy bacon. Every morning I slave away on websites.
